Our Creation

Giving Wings to your Stories

  • Our Creations, as the edgy name suggests, is a young and Creative venture by Chaitanya Gaidhani. With this move, the country’s most prolific movie production house enters the world of ad film production and the dynamic world of digital. Having occupied a distinct place in Nashik's Theater and in the hearts of millions of people with large scale dramas to coming-of-age stories, Our Creations has made versatility one of its key USPs by staying relevant with the changing times. The birth of Our Creations is in line with this very versatility, as the new venture will not only produce ad films for television, but will also execute creative and clutter breaking solutions across digital space.
  • Helmed by the very proficient Chaitanya Gaidhani, Our Creations intends to bring the same level of aesthetic quality and signature professionalism of the movie production business. Comprising some of the sharpest minds and creative professionals in India today, is an impeccable value-add for any brand. Bolstered by an extremely talented in-house creative team (a rarity when to comes to ad production houses in India), Our Creations holds a competitive edge over other established players in its space. However, the company functions on an adaptive model, making it open to collaborating with external creative partners for executing ad films and other creative solutions for its diverse set of clients.
